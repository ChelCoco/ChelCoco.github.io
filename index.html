About Me

I was born and raised in Mexico for the first 6 years of my life and had the privilege to move with my family to the U.S, Belgium, and Singapore. Although all of these countries became my home, Mexico holds a special place in my heart and therefore the design of this site was inspired by the night sky and bright fireflies from my home town. As a self-taught person I decided to create this website to keep track of my projects and develop my web development skills. I'm currently a Customer Success Manager at a tech company based in Atlanta, Georgia. My responsibilities include retaining and growing the customer base, project management, sales, tech support, host live webinar trainings and creating Guest Satisfaction Survey layouts and stylesheets using HTML and CSS.
